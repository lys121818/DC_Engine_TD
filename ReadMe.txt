Three Buttons on the Screen.

First Button Plays the music.
Second Button Spawn Enemy.
Third Button to exit the application.

Arrow Up and Down key to change size of the volume
Press P to pause the music it's playing and P again to resume

Game
Press W,A,S,D to move the player
left click to fire a bullet
When player get close to the enemy, the enemy will track player and shoots bullet.
When Enemy collide with bullet, the enemy get destroyed.
Collide with gray box zone on the right bottom to end game.
